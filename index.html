Welcome

Ski directly to and from your front door

Copper Cup Cabin sits right on the mountain — step out of your skis at the door, or wander five flat minutes into the village for dinner. Inside, heated floors and a soaking hot tub wait for you after a day on the hill, with views that stretch over the Okanagan's snow-laden pines from nearly every room.

Getting Around

Right where the mountain happens

Ski in, ski out, and everything else is a short walk away — the village, the Nordic trails, and Happy Valley's tubing and skating.

The cabin sits within Feathertop Estates, regarded as Big White's premier, luxury ski-in/ski-out neighbourhood.
