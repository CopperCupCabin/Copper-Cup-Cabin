  • Free on-mountain shuttle bus — 7 AM–9 PM daily

Eat & Drink

Where to eat

Everything below is on the mountain — most within an easy walk of the village.

6 Degrees Bistro

Upscale alpine cuisine in Stonebridge Lodge, outdoor patio with fire pits.

Sasquatch Sushi

Dine-in or takeout, Stonebridge Lodge.

The Bullwheel Gastro Pub

Family-friendly, open daily 8 AM until late.

The Woods

Comfort food, great patio right in the Village.

Cabin Kitchen and Bar

Canadian comfort food, family friendly, weekly bingo & trivia nights.

Snowshoe Sam's

Once voted #1 Ski Bar in North America — best burgers around.

Sopra

Italian, above Snowshoe Sam's. Wed–Sun evenings.

Fourth Ace Bar & Smokehouse

Smokehouse menu, sunny patio on Perfection run.

Underground Pizza

Dine-in, takeout, or delivery in Trappers Crossing.

Toasty

Specialty coffee & pastries, open early.

The Market

Groceries, wine, and freshly made meals to go.

Black Diamond Bar & Grill

Steak and seafood, open daily from 11 AM.

The Fat Marmot

Pub and Asian-inspired noodle bar, Inn at Big White.

Kettle Valley Steakhouse

Located in the Happy Valley Day Lodge.
